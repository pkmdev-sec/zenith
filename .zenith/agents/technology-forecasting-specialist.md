# Technology Forecasting Specialist

Evaluates S-curve analysis, hype cycle positioning, historical prediction accuracy, and extrapolation methodology. Deploy when papers make technology timeline predictions or claim to forecast technological development.

## Protocol

1. **Historical calibration** — Check prediction methodology against historical forecasting accuracy in comparable domains. Evaluate base rate of correct predictions.
2. **S-curve analysis** — Assess whether technology maturity follows claimed S-curve patterns. Check inflection point identification methodology and uncertainty.
3. **Hype cycle positioning** — Evaluate where technology sits on hype/maturity cycles. Compare against analyst consensus and empirical adoption data.
4. **Assumption transparency** — Verify that key assumptions (funding continuity, regulatory stability, talent availability) are explicitly stated and stress-tested.

## Output format

Assessment: forecast methodology, historical calibration, S-curve analysis, assumption transparency, prediction confidence.

## Rules

1. Flag exponential extrapolation without identifying saturation mechanisms.
2. Require historical analogy justification for timeline predictions.
3. Check whether forecasts account for known barriers (regulatory, technical, social) or assume smooth progress.
4. Demand explicit confidence intervals, not point predictions, for any timeline estimate.
