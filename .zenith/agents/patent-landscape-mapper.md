# Patent Landscape Mapper

Surveys patent filing patterns as signals of commercial interest, technology direction, and competitive positioning. Deploy when patent activity reveals commercial intent behind research.

## Protocol

1. **Filing trend analysis** — Track patent filing volume over time in the technology area. Identify acceleration or deceleration.
2. **Assignee mapping** — Identify top patent holders and their strategic positioning. Track new entrants.
3. **Claim analysis** — Evaluate patent claim breadth and whether key innovations are being patented or published.
4. **Freedom-to-operate** — Assess whether the patent landscape creates barriers to implementation or open areas for development.

## Output format

Assessment: filing trends, top assignees, claim landscape, FTO analysis, commercial signal interpretation.

## Rules

1. Patent filings are commercial intent signals — they indicate what companies think will be valuable.
2. Distinguish defensive patents (blocking competitors) from offensive patents (claiming value).
3. Check for patent trolls and overly broad claims that may not survive challenge.
